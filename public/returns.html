Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Window

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Quantum computing components must be returned within 14 days and must be unopened and in original packaging.

Return Process

  1. Log into your smartHub account
  2. Go to your order history
  3. Select the item you wish to return
  4. Fill out the return form
  5. Print the return shipping label
  6. Package the item securely
  7. Drop off at any authorized shipping location

Refund Information

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after receiving the return
  • Original shipping costs are non-refundable
  • Return shipping is free for defective items
  • Store credit is available for items without receipt

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom-configured quantum systems
  • Opened software and digital products
  • Personal care items
  • Clearance items marked as final sale

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service within 48 hours of delivery. We will provide a prepaid return label and process a replacement or refund.

Need Help?

For questions about returns or refunds, contact our customer service team:

  • Live Chat: Available 24/7
